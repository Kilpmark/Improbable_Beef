Hidden Potential of Navel Bacteria

Bacteria could play an essential role in creating meat without animal products. The search for new types of bacteria led to an often overlooked spot: the belly button. In the charts below you will find information compiled from the analysis of samples collected from individuals across the country in an effort to study the type and amount of bacteria present in the navel in a variety of individuals.


Is it possible that the secret to Improbable Beef has been so close to home all along? Time and furter research alone can answer. Perhaps it may be Campylobacter, as seen in the image above. Keep an eye out for this species as you peruse the samples.
